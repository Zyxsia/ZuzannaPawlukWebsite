About

Hi! My name is Zuzanna and I'm currently studying 3D Animation at Howest University in Belgium. I'm from Poland, where I attended an IT-focused technical high school. Since then, I have continuously developed my skills in graphics and animation, exploring new techniques and tools. Outside of creating graphics, I enjoy horse riding, traveling, reading books and watercolor painting.
